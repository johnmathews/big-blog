Summer children <br></br>
squeal and shriek, <br></br>
Watch wars. <br></br>
Excitement mounts. <br></br>

How exotic. <br></br>
Unexpectedly, <br></br>
Death arrives. <br></br>

Grow-up<br></br>
Grow old<br></br>
Face death <br></br>
Evade evil.<br></br>

Don't tire. <br></br>
World is fragile.<br></br>
Towers burn,<br></br>
Bridges bludgeon,<br></br>
Markets stab. <br></br>

Got to get out.
